Question 1
What were your key accomplishments / what are you most proud of achieving over the past year? *

When I joined as Chief of Staff, my role was to support the Head of GTM with operational coordination—running meetings, tracking action items, and managing communications. Over the course of 2025, I identified a structural gap in how Eudia captures and flows customer intelligence, and I took ownership of building the infrastructure to solve it.

This work expanded significantly beyond my original scope:

  • Built and deployed GTM Brain — a production intelligence platform connecting Slack, Obsidian, Outlook calendars, and Salesforce. This system is now live and used daily by the sales team, enabling natural language queries, automated meeting prep, and real-time pipeline visibility.
  • Developed Salesforce automation — custom Apex classes and Flows that auto-create Delivery records at Stage 4 and sync contract data at Close Won. This removes the manual data entry burden from Business Leads post-deal.
  • Implemented AI-powered capabilities — meeting classification, note summarization, and contract extraction using Claude and OpenAI APIs. BLs can now transcribe meetings directly in Obsidian, with AI generating structured summaries, MEDDICC signals, and next steps that sync to Salesforce.
  • Created BL performance infrastructure — custom Salesforce objects tracking time-to-ramp, monthly productivity, and YTD/QTD ACV, with weekly automated reports to leadership.

Measurable business impact since September 2025:

  • $6M+ in closed revenue
  • $16M+ in LOIs signed
  • 30 new logos closed

While I don't take credit for closing these deals—that's the work of our Business Leads—the infrastructure I built enabled the clean data capture, pipeline visibility, and operational efficiency that supported this velocity. The team is now operating with systems that didn't exist 12 months ago.

I'm most proud that this work aligns directly with the Revenue Operations Lead responsibilities Eudia posted 6 months ago—systems design, process automation, analytics infrastructure, and enabling field productivity. I identified where I could add the most value and executed with autonomy.
