Buying Triggers

M&A Activity

Announced acquisition, merger, or carve-out. Due diligence pain is acute and time-boxed.

Geographic Expansion

New markets (EMEA, APAC) create compliance complexity and contract volume spikes.

Funding Round

Series C+ indicates growth pressure, budget availability, and need to scale legal without linear headcount.

New CLO/GC Hire

New leader = mandate to show impact. 90-day window for quick wins.

CLM Fatigue

Implemented CLM 18+ months ago, underwhelmed by adoption. Looking for "what's next."

Industry Targeting & Account Intelligence
Pharma & Life Sciences Tier 1
Why target: Highest regulatory burden in any industry. FDA, EMA, and global compliance creates constant legal ops pressure. Heavy reliance on outside counsel for regulatory filings, clinical trial agreements, and IP. M&A consolidation continues—every deal = 6-8 week diligence cycles. Legal teams are cost centers under CFO scrutiny.
Pfizer Johnson & Johnson Merck AbbVie Bristol-Myers Squibb Eli Lilly Amgen Gilead
Lead with: Compliance automation + M&A due diligence speed. Reference 40-70% OC reduction on regulatory work.
Oil & Gas / Energy Tier 1
Why target: Massive contract volumes—JV agreements, drilling contracts, land rights, environmental compliance, vendor MSAs across global operations. Highly regulated (EPA, state agencies, international). Energy transition creating new deal complexity (renewables, carbon credits). Mature legal ops teams with budget authority.
ExxonMobil Chevron ConocoPhillips Schlumberger Halliburton Baker Hughes Phillips 66 Marathon
Lead with: Contract velocity + compliance automation. Emphasize global ops complexity and outside counsel consolidation.
Insurance Tier 1
Why target: State-by-state regulatory complexity multiplies compliance burden. High policy/contract volume with tight turnaround expectations. Claims litigation creates constant document review. M&A activity ongoing (consolidation + InsurTech acquisitions). Legal ops maturity varies—opportunity to modernize.
State Farm Liberty Mutual Travelers Chubb Hartford Allstate Progressive Nationwide
Lead with: Compliance + litigation support. Multi-state regulatory tracking and claims document review.
Manufacturing Tier 2
Why target: Global supply chain = thousands of vendor contracts annually. Trade compliance (tariffs, export controls) intensifying. ESG/sustainability compliance requirements emerging. OEMs face product liability and IP protection needs. Legal teams often lean—high OC dependency.
Caterpillar 3M Honeywell Ecolab Deere & Co Parker Hannifin Illinois Tool Works Emerson
Lead with: Vendor contracting velocity + trade compliance. Reference Ecolab: 60% OC reduction in 90 days.
Financial Services & PE Tier 2
Why target: Heavy regulatory burden (SEC, FINRA, OCC). M&A is core business for PE firms—diligence speed = competitive advantage. Portfolio company legal ops often fragmented. Banks face vendor management and contract standardization challenges. Compliance costs under constant scrutiny.
BlackRock KKR Carlyle Apollo TPG Ares US Bank PNC
Lead with: M&A diligence speed (6 weeks → 10 days) + portfolio company legal ops. Compliance for banks.
Technology / SaaS Tier 3
Why target: High-velocity contracting is core to revenue. Legal is often seen as "blocking deals." Growth-stage companies scaling without proportional legal headcount. Enterprise sales = complex negotiations, custom terms. Data privacy (GDPR, CCPA) adds compliance layer.
Salesforce ServiceNow Workday Snowflake Datadog CrowdStrike Palo Alto Zscaler
Lead with: Contract turnaround speed (14 days → 3 days). Revenue enablement angle—unblock deals faster.
