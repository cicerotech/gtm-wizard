Welcome to the Eudia GTM System

Your playbook for pipeline management, meeting prep, and closing deals efficiently. Get set up in minutes.

Setup Time: ~10 minutes
1

Why This System?

Zero Manual Data Entry
Meeting notes auto-sync to accounts. Calendar events flow to Salesforce. Spend time selling, not logging.
Meeting Prep in Seconds
Every meeting gets auto-generated context: prior notes, open opps, key contacts, and next steps.
Accurate Forecasting
Weekly updates drive EOQ projections, product traction insights, and resource planning.
Bottom line: This system removes friction from your workflow while ensuring leadership has the visibility they need. Update 7 fields weekly, and the rest handles itself.

Create Your Vault

When Obsidian opens, click "Create new vault" and name it (e.g., "Eudia Notes"). Choose a location on your computer.

Settings ‚åò , ‚Üí Community Plugins ‚Üí Turn on ‚Üí Browse ‚Üí Search "Scribe" ‚Üí Install ‚Üí Enable

Configure Scribe with OpenAI Key

Settings ‚Üí Scribe ‚Üí Paste the API key (provided by RevOps) ‚Üí Select "GPT-4o" model
Once configured, Scribe records audio ‚Üí transcribes ‚Üí generates AI summary with action items automatically.

Recording a Meeting

Open or create a note for the meeting. Click Scribe's record button in the ribbon. When done, Scribe transcribes and generates your summary.

Calendar context: Your calendar is already synced. Use the Meeting Prep tab to see context (prior notes, opps, contacts) before calls. Obsidian is for capturing notes during/after ‚Äî Meeting Prep provides the prep before.
