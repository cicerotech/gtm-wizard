Business Lead Email Templates

Omar-style: Direct, brief, outcome-focused | For BL use with prospects

Style Guide: Keep emails to 4-6 sentences. Lead with value. Be direct. Reference real customers. One clear ask. No jargon. Sign "Best, [Your Name]" for initial outreach, just "[Your Name]" for follow-ups.

[Your Name]

Variable Quick Reference

Common Variables
  • [COMPANY]: Prospect company name
  • [First Name]: Decision maker first name (always capitalize properly)
  • [TRIGGER]: Funding, acquisition, expansion, leadership change
  • [COMPARABLE]: Similar customer - match industry/size
  • [OUTCOME]: Specific metric with number (e.g., "60% OC reduction")
  • [PAIN]: Specific problem they've mentioned or you've identified
  • [TIMEFRAME]: Days/weeks for deployment and outcomes
  • [SPECIFIC ASK]: Clear next step (e.g., "30-min call Tuesday?")
Style Checklist (Before Sending):
  • ✅ 4-6 sentences max for cold outreach, 2-3 for follow-ups
  • ✅ One clear call-to-action
  • ✅ Specific numbers and companies (not vague claims)
  • ✅ No jargon ("leverage," "synergy," "solution," etc.)
  • ✅ Active voice, present tense
  • ✅ Peer-to-peer tone (not vendor-to-buyer)
  • ✅ Aligned with 2025 positioning: Outcomes > Tools, Built for CLOs, Company Brain
