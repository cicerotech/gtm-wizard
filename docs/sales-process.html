2. Qualification Criteria
Urgency is key: An opportunity should only move to Stage 2 (SQO) if the prospect is likely to transact within the next 90 days.

Qualification Requirements

  • Timeline: Prospect is likely to transact within 90 days
  • Budget: Budget confirmed or identifiable
  • Need: Clear use case and pain point identified
  • Champion: Internal advocate identified

Stage 2 Entry Requirement

Required: When an opportunity moves to Stage 2 (SQO), the BL must post a qualification justification/update in Slack #gtm-account-planning.

Why This Matters
Data accuracy drives business outcomes. Keeping your opportunities up-to-date is critical for three reasons:
  • Revenue Forecasting: Your data feeds directly into leadership and investor reporting. Accurate ACV, stage, and target dates enable reliable forecasting.
  • Correct Revenue Attribution: Proper tagging of new business vs. renewal ensures we capture opportunity amounts correctly and report accurately to stakeholders.
  • Product Traction Insights: Understanding which product lines gain early traction helps us drive more revenue, identify expansion opportunities, and inform product strategy.

When data is stale or incomplete, forecasts miss, investor reports are unreliable, and we lose visibility into what's working.
