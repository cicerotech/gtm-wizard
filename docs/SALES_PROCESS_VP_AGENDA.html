Gate 2: Stage 0 ‚Üí Stage 1

Proposed Gate Criteria
Stage 1 = Discovery/qualification meeting scheduled with right contact level (CLO/GC/Director+)
Pre-Qualification Required
  • ICP fit confirmed
  • Right contact level engaged
  • Basic pain/interest identified
Stage 1 Entry
  • Meeting on calendar (not just "let's find time")
  • Meeting scheduled date captured
  • Contact role documented
Decisions Needed
Block Stage 1 until meeting is on calendar? (Hard gate vs. soft reminder)

Stage 1 Activities & Demo Policy

Stage 1 Standards
1-2 qualification meetings maximum. MEDIC criteria assessment. NO full demos until qualified.
Stage 1 Activities
  • Discovery meeting(s)
  • Champion identification
  • Use case definition
  • MEDIC qualification
Demo Policy
  • Shadow demos / high-level overviews OK
  • Full product demos = Stage 2 only
  • Demo scheduled = Stage 2 gate passed
Decisions Needed
Hard-stop demos until Stage 2? (Enforce via validation rule)
Meeting count threshold that signals stuck deal? (>3 meetings in Stage 1)
Require MEDIC fields populated before Stage 2? (M, E, I, C minimum)

Multi-Product Opportunity Management

Core Question
When discussing Sigma, Insights, Contracting, Managed Services in one deal ‚Äî split or bundle?
Option A: Split at Stage 2
  • Qualify each product line separately
  • Create individual opps at Stage 2
  • Clear product line attribution
  • More opps to manage
Option B: Bundle with Attribution
  • Keep as single opportunity
  • Require $ attribution per product line
  • Use multi-select for products
  • Simpler to manage, harder to track
Decisions Needed
Split or Bundle? (If split: at what stage?)
If bundled: require $ attribution per product line?
Does AE comp structure influence this?
