2. Qualification Criteria (MEDDICC)
Urgency is key: Opportunity moves to Stage 2 (SQO) only if prospect is likely to transact within 90 days.
  • Metrics: What measurable outcomes expected?
  • Economic Buyer: Who controls budget?
  • Decision Criteria: What drives their decision?
  • Decision Process: Steps/approvals required?
  • Identify Pain: What problem are we solving?
  • Champion: Who is the internal advocate?
  • Competition: Who else is being evaluated?
