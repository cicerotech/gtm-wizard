Tips for Best Results

Be specific. "Write a LinkedIn post about compliance for insurance CLOs" works better than "write a post."
Ask follow-ups. After any response, you can ask "make it shorter" or "now draft an email version" or "what industry data can I add?"
Reference our products by name. Say "AI Contracting" or "AI Compliance" -- the AI knows exactly what these are and how to position them.
Chain tasks together. "Plan the campaign, then write the first email, then draft the LinkedIn post" -- the AI keeps context across the conversation.

Troubleshooting

The AI doesn't seem to know about Eudia

Make sure you opened the marketing-caroline folder (not a file inside it). Go to File → Open Folder and select the folder itself. The AI context loads from the .cursor/rules/ folder inside it.

Responses are generic or too long

Add specifics: target persona, industry, product, or campaign type. The more specific your prompt, the more targeted the output.

I want to create a Salesforce campaign from Cursor

This requires connecting Salesforce MCP (coming soon). For now, you can ask the AI to draft the campaign details and you create it manually in Salesforce -- the "New Campaign" button is on your Campaigns tab.

The AI gave me wrong information about a competitor

Always verify specific claims (funding amounts, product features) before using them externally. The AI searches the web but can occasionally get details wrong.
