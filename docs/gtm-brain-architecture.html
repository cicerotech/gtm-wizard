How GTM Brain Supports Your Pipeline
Early → Mid Stage
Building the Foundation
  • Account ownership queries for top-of-funnel accounts—instant clarity on who owns what
  • Account context provided: "What do we know about this client?" returns a summary if it exists in our systems
  • Proactively update CRM—add notes, reminders, create new opportunities
  • Mid-week or post-meeting updates can action pipeline adjustments, saving time in Thursday reviews
  • Reassign or transition accounts; initial assignment based on coverage + geography
  • Meetings held with accounts → BLs use Hyprnote → GTM Brain syncs notes to Salesforce accounts
Mid → Close
Accuracy & Handoff
  • Continued logging of notes via Einstein Activity Capture for meetings + emails
  • Notes synced from Hyprnote if not shared in GTM account planning
  • At Stage 4: Delivery object created and associated with the opportunity
  • Weekly reports shared with leadership for visibility into pipeline movement, late-stage deals, and resource planning needs
  • At Closed Won: Deal terms, product lines, ACV, TCV captured and validated
  • Syncs to Rocket Lane where reps interact and update for internal/external use

One integration layer connecting Slack questions, meeting notes, and GTM resources to clean Salesforce data, finance reconciliation in Campfire, and live dashboards—even for teammates without Salesforce access.
