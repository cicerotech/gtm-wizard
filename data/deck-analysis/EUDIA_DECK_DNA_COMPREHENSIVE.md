## 8. Deck Generation Ruleset

### Required Elements (Every Deck)

1. **Title Slide** with clear outcome-oriented headline
2. **Credibility Slide** - funding, investors, F500 logos
3. **Problem Framing** - market context + specific pain points
4. **Solution Overview** - How Eudia Delivers Outcomes framework
5. **Company Brain Explanation** - core differentiator
6. **At least 2 Proof Points** with quantified metrics
7. **Customer Quote** from C-level executive
8. **Close/Next Steps** slide

### Slide Count Guidelines

- **Overview/Pitch Deck:** 10-12 slides
- **Case Study Focus:** 12-15 slides
- **Pricing/Commercial:** 2-3 slides (keep brief)

### Tone & Voice

- **Confident but not arrogant:** "proven track record," "measurable outcomes"
- **Outcome-focused:** Always tie features to business results
- **Enterprise-appropriate:** Professional, sophisticated, not startup-casual
- **Specific over vague:** Numbers, names, titles, timelines
