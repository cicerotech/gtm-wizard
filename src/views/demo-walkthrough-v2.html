Admin Overhead

We built an intelligence layer that fixes this. Not a new tool reps have to learn -- it works behind the scenes, connecting what already exists.

Record & Capture
One-click meeting recording with AI-powered transcription
Open any account folder, click the mic icon, select your meeting type -- and the note auto-populates with the right template before you even start. When the call ends, AI generates a structured summary with MEDDICC signals, next steps, and a draft follow-up email tailored to the meeting type.

Record: Click mic, select template (Sales Discovery, Demo, CS, General, Internal). Note pre-populates with sections.

Organized: Each account has Contacts, Intelligence, Meeting Notes, Next Steps -- all synced from Salesforce.

  • Five meeting templates: Sales Discovery (MEDDIC), Demo, Customer Success, General, Internal
  • AI transcription with speaker labels, key quotes, and action items extracted automatically
  • Draft follow-up email generated per template -- Demo emails reference feature reactions, MEDDIC emails reference pain points

Contacts: Auto-enriched from Salesforce with names, titles, emails. Always current.
