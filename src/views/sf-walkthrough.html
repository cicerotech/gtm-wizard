How It All Connects

From prospect to closed deal to delivery. Here's the full lifecycle of a deal in our Salesforce.

Account → Opportunity → Work the Deal → Delivery (S4) → Contract (Won) → Revenue
Step 1
The Account
Every company is an Account. Contacts, opportunities, CSM notes, and account health -- all on one page.

Contacts

Key stakeholders with titles, emails, and roles. Add Contact Roles on Opportunities to track who's involved in each deal.

Opportunities

All active and closed deals for this company. Click any to see deal details.

CSM Section

Account health, health history, and customer overview managed by Customer Success.

Eudia Overview

Open ACV, total revenue, CLO engagement, marquee/velocity flags, first deal date.

Step 2
Creating an Opportunity
Click "New Opportunity" from the nav bar or any Account page. Fill in the required fields and hit Create.

Account *

Search and select the company. Links the deal to the Account record.

Stage *

Start at S1 Discovery for new deals. Moves through S2 SQO, S3 Pilot, S4 Proposal, S5 Negotiation.

Product Lines *

Check all products in scope: AI Contracting, Compliance, Sigma, Insights, Litigation, M&A, FDE, Secondee.

ACV + Target Date + Source

Annual value, when you expect to close, and how the deal originated (Inbound/Outbound/Events/Referral).

Step 3
Working the Deal
Your deal workspace. Stage progression bar at the top, key fields, product lines, and contact roles below.

Stage Bar

Visual progression from S1 to Closed Won. Click "Mark Stage as Complete" to advance. Current stage is highlighted.

Key Fields

ACV, Target Sign Date, Forecast Category (Pipeline/Gut/Commit), Next Steps, Days in Stage, BL Forecast.

Product Lines

Multi-select with individual products shown as cards. Each product creates a separate Delivery at S4.

Contact Roles

Add contacts from the Account who are involved in this deal. Track champions, economic buyers, and decision makers.

Step 4
MEDDICC + Next Steps
Track deal qualification and maintain a running log of customer history and next actions. Every update here feeds into Pipeline Review and GTM Brain.

Customer History + Next Steps is your running deal log. Add dated entries each time you engage. Format: "2026-02-10: NF - Demo scheduled for Tuesday 10th Feb." This content syncs to Pipeline Review's Next Steps column and is queryable by GTM Brain.

Contract Details (Won) section shows Products Breakdown with per-product ACV, Delivery links (DEL-0231, DEL-0232...), and total ACV. This auto-populates when the deal moves to Closed Won.

Step 5
Delivery (Auto-Created at S4)
When a deal reaches Stage 4 (Proposal), Deliveries auto-create -- one per product line. Multiple products = multiple Deliveries, each tracked separately.

One Delivery per Product

AppliedAI has 4 products, so 4 Deliveries were created: DEL-0231 (Sigma), DEL-0232 (Insights), DEL-0233 (Contracting), DEL-0234 (Compliance).

Delivery Details

Product Line, Delivery Model (AI-First 95%/5% Human), Phase (Month 1 - Setup), Status (Planning), Kickoff Date, Account link, Opportunity link.

Step 6
Contract (Closed Won)
When a deal closes, a Contract is created with line items, total value, term, and start/end dates. Links back to the Opportunity and Deliveries.

Contract Line Items

One per product: AI Contracting - Technology, AI Compliance - Technology, AI M&A - Managed Services. Each tracks quantity and value.

Term + Value

Contract Term (36 months), Total Contract Value ($600K), Start/End dates, Currency (USD), Contract Type (Recurring).

BOI Deal Code

Every Closed Won deal gets a unique BOI code (BOI-001, BOI-002...). Renewals inherit the parent code + suffix (BOI-001-01). Finance uses this for Campfire revenue mapping.

Renewal Tracking

The Renewal Opp field links renewal opportunities back to the original deal. BOI codes chain together for full revenue lineage.

Daily View
Pipeline Review
All active deals across the team with week-over-week change tracking. Filter by pod, stage, product, or target date. Click any cell to edit directly.

Inline editing: Click ACV, Target Date, Stage, FC, Products, or Next Steps to edit directly in the report. Changes save to Salesforce immediately. Collapse/expand rep sections by clicking the header bar.

Q1 Targeting
Accounts & Targets
Your Q1 account book. See assignments, outreach tiers (T1 Exec vs T2), geographic spread, and Eudia positioning context. Drop accounts to the pool or claim new ones.

Account Distribution

Every BL's Q1 book size by US/EU Pod. Front Book (pipeline + existing) vs Back Book (targeting). T1 Exec (leadership outreach) vs T2 (BL-driven).

Expanded View

Click your name to see every account with HQ, Industry, Revenue, Eudia Positioning Context, Status, Book, and Tier. Drop or Transfer from here.

Target Pool

700+ accounts available for claiming. Filter by Tier, Pod, Industry, State. Claim to add to your book (pod-restricted).

Weekly Focus

Recommended weekly targeting per rep. Accounts clustered by geography or industry. Swap accounts in/out of your weekly focus.

The Full Lifecycle

Account

Company record with contacts, history, and CSM health.

Opportunity

Deal tracked from S1 Discovery through S6 Closed Won.

Products

Multi-select product lines. Each creates a Delivery at S4.

Delivery

Auto-created at S4. One per product. Tracks phases + model.

Contract

Created on close. Line items, term, value, BOI code.

Revenue

BOI code maps to Campfire. Renewals chain via code suffix.
