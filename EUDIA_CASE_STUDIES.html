Using Case Studies in Sales Conversations

Sales Playbook: When to Share Which Case Study

Discovery Stage

  • Listen for pain points (outside counsel costs, slow turnaround, scaling challenges)
  • Map to case study with similar industry/challenge
  • Share in follow-up email with specific metrics that mirror their situation

Qualification Stage

  • Use case studies to establish credibility ("We've done this before")
  • Quantify outcomes to help them build internal business case
  • Reference comparable customers to create peer pressure/FOMO

Proposal Stage

  • Attach full case study as appendix to proposal
  • Use metrics to build ROI model for their specific situation
  • Offer customer reference call if case study resonates

Objection Handling

  • "Too expensive": Show payback period from case study (typically 3-4 months)
  • "We're using [LegalTech]": Show how Eudia delivered outcomes on top of similar tools
  • "Not ready for AI": Show speed of deployment (days not months)
  • "Need to prove ROI first": Reference pilot structure from case studies

Messaging Alignment Checklist

Before sending ANY email or case study, verify alignment with 2025 positioning:
  • ✅ Outcomes > Tools: Lead with business value (savings, speed, revenue), not features
  • ✅ Built for CLOs: Language positions us as in-house allies, not vendors or consultants
  • ✅ Company Brain: Emphasize our platform creates institutional intelligence, not just one-time automation
  • ✅ 2026 Context: Frame urgency around market shift from AI "exploration" to execution with ROI
  • ✅ Speed: Highlight deployment in days/weeks, outcomes within 30-90 days (vs. quarters/years for alternatives)
  • ✅ Forward-Deployed Engineers: Emphasize human-led transformation, not just software deployment
